About Me

I began working in construction at the age of 14, gaining experience across a wide range of trades. Early on, I discovered a passion for building and creating, which has stayed with me throughout my life. My first job was selling newspaper subscriptions, but I always gravitated toward hands-on work and problem-solving.

As an adult, my interest in technology grew alongside my love for video games and storytelling. I began experimenting with amateur coding and game design, turning a hobby into a serious pursuit. I realized that the skills I honed in construction—planning, building, and creating—could translate into building websites, apps, and even games.

After facing significant challenges in 2024, including job loss and homelessness with my family, I found support through the Orange County Rescue Mission. This experience gave me the opportunity to return to education, earn my GED, and enroll in an online coding bootcamp. What started as a hobby has now become my profession, and I am passionate about continuing to grow as a developer and creator.

Skills

  • React / Frontend Development
  • Node.js / Backend Development
  • Git / GitHub
  • Problem-solving and project planning

Interests

Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring creative projects, designing video games, reading, and building solutions that challenge me to learn and grow. I am driven by curiosity, creativity, and the desire to turn ideas into tangible results.
